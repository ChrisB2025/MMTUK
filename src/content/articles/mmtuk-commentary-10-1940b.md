#### Challenging Growth Paradigms

Traditional notions of economic growth often overlook the importance of well-being and sustainability. This commentary challenges the prevailing paradigms, advocating for a broader understanding of what constitutes true progress in economic terms. By redefining growth, we can align economic policies with societal needs.
