#### Debt Dynamics

National debt is often viewed negatively, yet it can play a crucial role in fostering economic growth. This research delves into how strategic debt management can stimulate investment and innovation, ultimately benefiting the economy. Understanding these dynamics is essential for effective fiscal policy.
