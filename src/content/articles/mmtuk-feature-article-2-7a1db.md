#### Government Intervention

In times of economic downturn, government intervention becomes essential. By increasing public spending, governments can stimulate demand and support recovery. This approach not only aids in immediate relief but also lays the groundwork for sustainable growth. Understanding the mechanisms of fiscal policy is crucial for effective economic management.
