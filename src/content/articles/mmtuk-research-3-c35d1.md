#### Equity in Fiscal Policy

Fiscal policy plays a pivotal role in addressing social inequalities. By analyzing tax structures and public spending, we can identify how policies can be designed to promote equity. This research highlights the importance of inclusive economic strategies that benefit all segments of society, ensuring that fiscal measures contribute to social justice.
