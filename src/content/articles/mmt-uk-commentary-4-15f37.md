#### Public Finance Evolution

The landscape of public finance is evolving, influenced by the principles of MMT. As governments reconsider their roles in the economy, the focus shifts towards sustainable funding models that prioritize public welfare. This commentary reflects on the potential transformations in fiscal policy and their implications for future economic stability.
