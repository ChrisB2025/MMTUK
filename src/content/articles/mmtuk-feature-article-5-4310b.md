#### Reassessing Debt

National debt and deficits are often viewed with skepticism. However, MMT offers a different lens through which to assess these concepts. By understanding that sovereign nations can issue currency, we can rethink the implications of debt. This article delves into the nuances of fiscal responsibility and the potential for innovative economic policies.
