#### Equity in Fiscal Policy

Fiscal policy plays a pivotal role in addressing social inequalities. By reallocating resources through taxation and public spending, governments can promote social equity. This research highlights the importance of understanding how fiscal measures can be designed to support marginalized communities and foster inclusive growth.
