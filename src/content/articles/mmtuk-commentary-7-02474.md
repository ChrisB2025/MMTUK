#### Economic Literacy

Understanding economic principles is crucial for informed citizenship. This commentary highlights the importance of economic literacy in navigating complex financial landscapes. By fostering a better understanding of economic systems, we empower individuals to engage in meaningful discussions about fiscal policies and their societal impacts.
