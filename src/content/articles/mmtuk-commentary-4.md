#### Public Spending and Inflation

Concerns about inflation often arise when discussing increased public spending. However, MMT posits that inflation is not solely a result of government expenditure. Instead, it is influenced by various factors, including supply chain dynamics and market conditions. Understanding these nuances is essential for informed economic policy.
