# Introducing Our New Training Program

MMT UK is excited to launch a new training program aimed at enhancing skills in the workforce. This program will cover various topics, including leadership, project management, and digital skills.

## Program Details

The training sessions will be held monthly, starting in November 2023. Participants will receive hands-on experience and valuable resources to apply in their roles.
