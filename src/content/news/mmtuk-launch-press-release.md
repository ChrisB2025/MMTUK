## MMTUK Launches as UK's Dedicated Modern Monetary Theory Research Organisation

**London, 5 February 2026** – MMTUK today announces its official launch as an independent research organisation dedicated to advancing public understanding of Modern Monetary Theory (MMT) and its policy implications for the United Kingdom.

## About MMTUK

MMTUK brings together leading academics, researchers and advocates to provide rigorous, evidence-based analysis of UK economic policy through the lens of Modern Monetary Theory. Our mission is to equip policymakers, civil society and the public with the tools to understand how money, taxation and government spending actually work.

## Our Work

MMTUK will focus on:

- **Academic Research**: Peer-reviewed studies translating macroeconomic insights into actionable UK policy proposals
- **Education**: Resources and training to build economic literacy across the UK
- **Community Building**: A nationwide network of local groups connecting MMT advocates

## Founding Statement

"Understanding how money actually works is essential for informed democratic participation," said Patricia Pino, Executive Director of MMTUK. "We're launching MMTUK to ensure that evidence-based economic analysis reaches those who need it most – from policymakers to the general public."
