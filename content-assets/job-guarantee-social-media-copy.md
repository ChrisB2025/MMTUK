### LinkedIn
> Today MMT UK publishes our first policy paper — A Counter-Inflationary Job Guarantee for the United Kingdom — alongside a 15-minute film introducing the proposal.
>
> Most people don't realise that governments deliberately maintain a pool of unemployed people as their primary tool for controlling inflation. This paper proposes a better way.
>
> The Job Guarantee offers employment to anyone willing and able to work. It replaces unemployment as a price anchor with a labour standard — a paid employment buffer that expands during downturns and contracts as the private sector recovers.
>
> The film features insights from:
> - Pavlina Tcherneva (Bard College / Levy Economics Institute) — author of The Case for a Job Guarantee
> - Daniel Kostzer (International Trade Union Confederation) — member of the team that designed Argentina's Plan Jefe employment guarantee
> - Patricia Pino (MMT UK) — lead author of the paper
>
